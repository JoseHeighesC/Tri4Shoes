Kids Shoes Fundraiser

About the Initiative

Hello, I am starting a fundraiser to provide athletic shoes to children from low-income families in the Peru. The initiative works as follows: for each event I participate in, you can pledge a specific amount to donate. After the event, 100% of your contribution will go directly toward purchasing sports shoes for children in need. Each shoe costs either $24 or $30 to produce depending on the size and model. Shipping is covered.

About Me

My name is Pepe. In June, I started an internship in a small town in Pennsylvania, and I found myself with a lot of free time. I decided to start training for a triathlon. After a few weeks, I realized that not only could I work toward this personal goal, but I could also use the opportunity to help others. That’s why I started this fundraiser, to provide sports shoes to children from low-income families who can’t afford them. I believe every child deserves the chance to play, run, and explore with proper footwear that supports their health and well-being.
